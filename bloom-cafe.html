Our Story

A Place to Slow Down & Savour

Born from a love of good coffee and unhurried mornings, Bloom Café opened in 2019 with one simple belief — that breakfast and brunch should feel like a celebration, not an afterthought.

Every dish is made from scratch with locally sourced ingredients, and every cup is brewed by our trained baristas using single-origin beans we select personally.
